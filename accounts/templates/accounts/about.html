About us

Inspired by the confines of a Gramercy studio and NYU dormitory life during the pandemic, StudyCity was built for those seeking more tailored options to study and work throughout New York City’s five boroughs. The application was designed to give users the ability to filter location-based search results to their preferences using rich contextual information about local study spaces through a seamless integration of data from Yelp!, user reviews and ratings, and NYC OpenData. StudyCity also supports business users who wish to claim their location as a verified StudySpace and advertise in local search results.

Our talented team came together while taking Software Engineering I at NYU’s Tandon School of Engineering in Brooklyn during the Fall 2021 semester. We set out with a common vision to build a unified tool for the university student, the home-based worker, the remote freelancer – and anyone else looking for an inspirational space to study, read a book, write some code, or enjoy a caffeinated beverage. We hope you register an account and enjoy the experience! Please feel free to check out the team’s LinkedIn and GitHub profiles linked below!
